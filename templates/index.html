Quienes Somos?

We are a Pentecostal church rooted in the vibrant community of Atlantic City. Our faith is deeply anchored in Pentecostal beliefs, emphasizing the transformative power of the Holy Spirit. As a welcoming and diverse congregation, we come together to worship, pray, and celebrate the presence of God in our lives. At our core, we believe in the gifts of the Spirit, fervent prayer, and the life-changing experience of being filled with the Holy Ghost. Our mission is to spread the love and teachings of Jesus Christ, embracing all who seek spiritual growth and connection in our beautiful coastal city.
